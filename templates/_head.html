{# Shared head fragment — meta + font loading. Included inside every template's via {% include '_head.html' %}. Fonts: preconnect to googleapis hosts (saves the DNS+TLS round trip) then a single with both families. Loading here (not via @import in base.css) lets the browser fetch the stylesheet in parallel with base.css instead of serially after it. `display=swap` makes the system fallback show immediately while webfonts download — eliminates the flash of invisible text. #}

Likely Duplicates

—
Activities sharing a date with another activity whose distance and duration both fall within ±5%. Same-source re-syncs (Strava re-export of a Garmin ride) usually match exactly. Use Exclude to keep a duplicate file but stop it counting in stats; Delete archives the GPX to your backups folder.

Odd Times

—
Activities whose start time (in the ride's local timezone) falls between 21:00 and 07:00. A diagnostic for the timezone-normalization fix — anything that lights up here either is a genuine night ride or has a timestamp that didn't get correctly localized at parse time.

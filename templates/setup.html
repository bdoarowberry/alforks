Set up Strava access (one time)
  1. Open strava.com/settings/api and create an app with these values:
    • Application Name: AlForks (or anything)
    • Category: Personal
    • Website: http://localhost
    • Authorization Callback Domain: localhost
  2. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret Strava shows you, paste them below, and Save.

Heart-rate zones are computed as percentages of your effective max HR:
  • Z1 Recovery — < 60%
  • Z2 Endurance — 60–70%
  • Z3 Tempo — 70–80%
  • Z4 Threshold — 80–90%
  • Z5 VO2max — > 90%
